# DoorDash - Food Delivery App

Welcome to the DoorDash project! This README provides an overview of the DoorDash app, its features, and instructions for setup.

## Project Overview

DoorDash is a popular food delivery app that connects people with the best in their cities. With a vast network of local restaurants and delivery drivers, DoorDash makes it easy for users to order their favorite meals and have them delivered right to their doorstep.

## Features

### 1. Restaurant Discovery
- Browse through a diverse range of local restaurants.
- Discover new cuisines and find your favorite dishes.

### 2. Easy Ordering
- Intuitive and user-friendly interface for seamless food ordering.
- Customize your orders with special instructions and dietary preferences.

### 3. Real-time Tracking
- Track the status of your order in real-time.
- Receive updates on the preparation, pickup, and delivery of your food.

### 4. Secure Payments
- Multiple payment options, including credit cards, digital wallets, and cash.
- Secure and encrypted transactions for user safety.

### 5. Scheduled Deliveries
- Plan your meals in advance by scheduling orders for later.
- Set delivery times that suit your convenience.

### 6. Ratings and Reviews
- Read restaurant reviews and ratings from other DoorDash users.
- Contribute your feedback to help others make informed decisions.

## Installation

To use DoorDash, follow these steps:

### For Mobile (iOS and Android)
1. Download the DoorDash app from the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/doordash/id719972451) (iOS) or [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.dd.doordash&hl=en&gl=US) (Android).
2. Install the app on your device.
3. Sign up or log in to your DoorDash account.
4. Start exploring restaurants and placing orders!

### For Web (DoorDash Website)
1. Visit the [DoorDash website](https://www.doordash.com/).
2. Sign up or log in to your DoorDash account.
3. Enter your delivery address to discover available restaurants.
4. Browse menus, customize your order, and proceed to checkout.
